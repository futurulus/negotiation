You and another user online want to set two of your friends up on a date. For each of your friends, you know their favorite hobby and their location/time preferences. Find the best match by chatting with your partner and finding the pair of friends who have the most in common! (Hint: There's exactly one pair that has all three in common.)

Instructions

  • Please use natural sentences as much as possible.
    • Do: i have 3 friends who like graffiti and being outdoors
    • Don't do: 3 graffiti outdoors
  • Avoid simply listing out the hobbies (or preferences) of your friends.
  • Look at your list of friends at right.
  • Use the chat box below to find out more about your partner's friends.
  • Once you've found the pair, select your friend by clicking Select in the first column.
  • Please select carefully. If you select a friend once, the website will not allow you to select another friend for 10 seconds.
